Palette

  1. Mocha Mousse
  2. Ethereal Blue

Pillar 1: Strategy and Blueprint

Research uncovers user pain points. Low-fidelity wireframing focuses on hierarchy and flow.

Pillar 2: Visual Design

The visual system uses Mocha Mousse for stability, Ethereal Blue for trust, and Moonlit Grey as a calm neutral.

Pillar 3: Implementation

The page is organized with semantic landmarks such as header, nav, main, article, aside, and footer.
